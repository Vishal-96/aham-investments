Driving Africa’s Connectivity

Aham's reach extends far beyond cotton processing. Our robust logistics network is a cornerstone of our brand, connecting communities and facilitating trade across East and Southern Africa. We proudly serve Kenya, Burundi, Rwanda, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Sudan, Zambia, Malawi, and of course, our home country Tanzania. Transportation has been a core competency for Aham for over two decades, forming the very foundation of our company.

Seamless Cross-Border Logistics

We specialize in seamless cross-border transportation, ensuring reliable and timely delivery of goods. Our team of highly skilled and efficient drivers possesses extensive knowledge of the region's roads and routes, ensuring your goods are in capable hands. In fact, Aham's founders began their journey by conquering the roads themselves, instilling a deep understanding of the transportation landscape within the company's DNA. Our fleet of trucks is meticulously maintained and secured. Before each departure, our experienced mechanics conduct thorough inspections to minimize potential complications and ensure a smooth journey.

Standing Strong with Communities

Aham's commitment to service and community extends beyond business. During the challenging times of the pandemic, we demonstrated our dedication and resilience by actively participating in food distribution efforts within Tanzania and neighboring countries. Even under drastic and demanding circumstances, Aham stepped up to provide essential support. We understand the importance of reliability and being there for our communities in times of need. It was a privilege for us to contribute to these efforts, reinforcing our belief that we are all in this together.
