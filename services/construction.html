Building Tanzania’s Future

Aham actively shapes Tanzania's landscape through its expertise in the vital field of construction. We offer a comprehensive range of heavy construction equipment, including excavators, tippers, cranes, compactors, graders, mixers, backhoes, and more, all at your service. Our team of expert civil engineers, each with over six years of experience, is ready to tackle any project, big or small.

Powering Communities

Aham has a proven track record of assisting both public and private entities with their construction needs. We've undertaken numerous projects across Tanzania that have positively impacted communities. One such initiative involved participating in a nationwide effort to expand electricity access, connecting communities and lighting up cities. This project, with a specific focus on the Ibadakuli Shinyanga Main Powerstation, aimed to bring power to all, fostering development and improving quality of life.

Shaping Infrastructure

Our contributions to infrastructure development don't stop there. Aham has also played a key role in connecting cities through extensive road construction projects, facilitating trade, travel, and national growth.

Likewise, we are proud to have also contributed to agricultural development by helping build a dam in Morogoro. This vital infrastructure project provides crucial water resources for irrigation, boosting agricultural productivity and supporting local communities

Our Construction Expertise

At Aham Investment Co. Ltd, we are committed to shaping a stronger and more connected Tanzania through innovation, precision, and integrity in construction. Our projects speak of reliability, craftsmanship, and vision.

1.

Roads, Dams, and Infrastructure

We work hand in hand with government agencies to develop key infrastructure projects — building roads, dams, and essential facilities that connect villages to towns and improve accessibility nationwide. Our goal is to make communities thrive and to play an active role in driving the nation’s growth and progress.

2.

Public Facilities

From modern stores and mosques to apartments and community structures, we have successfully delivered a range of public facilities. Guided by our director’s keen eye for design and quality, every project reflects durability, functionality, and aesthetic excellence.

3.

Machinery Hire Services

We provide reliable machinery hire services — all operated by skilled and experienced professionals. Our fleet includes excavators, graders, compactors, cranes, and more. Whether for small or large-scale projects, our equipment and operators are always ready to deliver efficient and timely results.

Residential Buildings

Our journey in residential construction is driven by passion. In 2018, our CEO brought his dream home to life — an experience that inspired our continued pursuit of excellence in homebuilding. At Aham Investment, we turn visions into realities. With our expertise in civil engineering, we are your one-stop solution for elegant, strong, and lasting homes.
