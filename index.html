Our Services

Cotton

Established in 2004, AHAM INVESTMENTS CO. LTD is committed to fulfilling the increasing demands for effective cotton ginning, and the transportation of a wide range of goods and commodities, catering to both local and international markets. We specialize in civil and structural engineering, prioritizing safety and environmental responsibility for sustainable development.

Our goal is to provide excellence, safety, and sustainability in all our operations, contributing to the future of infrastructure while supporting the textile industry and ensuring exceptional service quality.

Transportation

Our key services include:
• Ginning of Raw Cotton to Cotton Lint and Seeds
• Transportation of Goods by Road Within and Neighboring African Countries
• Importation and Supply of Essential Goods
• Civil and Building Engineering

Dedicated to the expert cultivation and processing of cotton, we uphold the highest quality benchmarks to maintain a sustainable supply chain.

Humanitarian Partnership with World Food Programme (WFP)

We proudly collaborate with the World Food Programme (WFP), contributing to global humanitarian efforts by ensuring reliable logistics and timely food assistance delivery. Our role in this initiative underscores our dedication to social responsibility and our commitment to improving lives through dependable supply chain operations.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, AHAM INVESTMENTS CO. LTD was among the few organizations in the region that remained fully operational. We ensured the uninterrupted supply of essential goods and services, supporting our workers’ livelihoods and maintaining stability for our partners and communities when it was needed most.
