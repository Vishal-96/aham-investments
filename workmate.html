About Us

Establishment

Workmate Network Limited is a private company founded on April 24, 2021, focused on delivering efficient and reliable services across mining, quarrying, and construction.

Our expertise includes stone crushing, screening, grading aggregates, building construction, finishing works, site preparation, demolition, and transportation of goods both locally and across borders.

Organisation & Management

The company is governed by a Board of Directors, responsible for planning, coordination, and overall control of operations.

Daily operations are led by the Managing Director Mr. Amir Ally Amir, alongside Mr. Hemed Amour Hamduni, who oversees quarry site management and execution.

Brief Details of Successfully Executed Projects

Lifewater International

Lifewater International, an NGO involved in water reservoir construction and supply management, has regularly partnered with us to supply gravel aggregate and quarry dust as per government standards. Using our own equipment and logistics, we ensured smooth project execution and successful delivery.

Jonta Investment Company Limited – Road Construction

We supply ballast aggregate for road construction projects under TANROAD standards. Our consistent supply and quality have helped ensure smooth progress and successful ongoing execution.

Shinyanga Municipal Council – Construction Supply

We provided high-quality gravel aggregate for building construction projects including offices and multi-storey structures. All materials passed strict government quality checks.

516KJ Kizumbi JWTZ – Barracks Renovation

We supplied ballast aggregate for multiple construction sites within military barracks in Shinyanga. Our reliability and timely delivery ensured successful completion of the project.
