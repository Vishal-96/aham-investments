Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 Dec, 2025

At AHAM INVESTMENTS CO. LTD, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us through our enquiry form.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information from you:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company / Address (if provided)
  • Any additional information voluntarily submitted in contact messages

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries or service requests
  • To provide you with relevant information about our services
  • For internal record-keeping and business operations
  • For marketing and communication purposes — Our team may use your contact details to reach out regarding services, updates, offers, or follow-ups related to AHAM Investments.

*You may request to stop receiving marketing communication at any time by contacting us.*

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement strong administrative and technical measures to ensure your data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

AHAM INVESTMENTS CO. LTD does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. We may only share your details:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in running our operations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our legal rights or prevent fraud

5. Cookies & Website Usage

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and website performance. Cookies do not give access to your personal data and may be disabled through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our records
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communication

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

AHAM INVESTMENTS CO. LTD may update this policy from time to time to reflect legal changes or improvements in our privacy practices. The latest version will always be available on this page.
